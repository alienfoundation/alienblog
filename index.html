The day I decided to overcome my fear of change and things were far better.

27th July, 2018

Alien

You know what I fear the most! "Change". Like this single word has always been the reason of my pain, anger, depression and most of the bad things that happen to me. I fear change a lot. Since the very start, I had very few to none friends because I used to live in my own world, I never tried to come out of my thoughts and see the real world. I was too scared to do so because my thoughts give me relief, they always give me peace. I know that even if I come out of my thoughts and encounter the real world, I can still go back anytime. But I don't wanted to change things. Everything was very smooth this way. I don't wanted to get that pain to deal with the society and the people around me. So I always chose to be alone.
This was just one example how fear of change always stopped me from doing anything, even if it was for my good. It used to get hurt, I was dying inside each and every single day but my fear stopped me from changing and with time I got used to it.

When I entered college, I thought that this time I will change the way I live, I will try to be a better person, a good friend, a good student etc. The start was good, but again the fear appeared that people will leave me because I think a lot different, because I am too weak to trust myself, because my thoughts are not congruent with the world etc. and always used to think that no one will understand why I was this way. No one ever did. But I forgot that people understand what I make them understand, people see what I show them, people act on how I act with them. The thought that the world will understand me without me speaking to anyone was shit. I got to take the initiative, if I wanted someone then I will have to show them. Just telling them and thinking that they will understand is not going to work. The world works very differently than what I used to think. I need to be in the system to understand the system. My thoughts can't change the system until I take actions. But again, taking actions give pain, and I was too comfortable in my thoughts to take that pain and change to fit in this society. But now or later, life teachs everyone that you will have to fit in the society to survive, harsh but true. Either you "accept the life the way it is and take actions to make it the way you want", or "just sit and think of the life you want". Choice is all yours but the later will do nothing except destroying you one day.

I used to think that I will lose my identity in the process of being better, in the process of changing myself for good. But life taught me that changing is not about losing you identity, changing is not about walking in someone elseâ€™s shoes instead change is all about you being more better, more stronger and more confident about your thoughts and the way you see the world. Change is about trusting yourself, change is about making your thoughts more positive, change is about trusting the people around you that they will not leave if I have trust on myself that I will never push them because I am too scared of losing them.

Fear will always push away the life that you want, it will always come into the way which leads to happiness. Change is the bridge between who you are and who you wanna be, change is the path to a better life, change is something you do when you know that you are killing yourself everyday by being the same. If you think you are not happy with your life, sit and talk to yourself and see what things are making you sad, if something is wrong then find why it is wrong and fix it and to do so you will have to change your habits. You deserve to live a happy life, don't screw up your life just because you are too scared to change.

"Change for the good. Change for yourself. Change if you are not happy with your life."
